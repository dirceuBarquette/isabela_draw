• download and unzip the package aanyware you wish
  • launch index.html

Usage

  • click with the left button of mouse in the "new block" icon
  • click with the left button of mouse in any place of the big gray area
    • in some seconds a white area will appear inside its
  • choose the color clicking on with the left button of mouse in the "CP" (ColorPicker) icon
  • click with the left button of mouse in the "draw with pencil" icon
  • after that, click with the left button of mouse in any place of the white area

now you already can draw on the web!!!!

  • to change the color or to add a block,
    click with the left button of mouse once more and repeat the operation

enjoy!!!
